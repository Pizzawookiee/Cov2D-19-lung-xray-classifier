Cov2D-19 Lung X-Ray Disease Classifier

Why Is This Tool Important?

Lung X-Ray scans are a common way of diagnosing severe respiratory illnesses. With hospitals at or near full capacity, it is important for doctors to be able to accurately determine if a patient is sick.

What Does This Do?

This machine learning model (a 2D convolutional neural network utilizing the DenseNet architecture through transfer learning) takes in X-Ray images and classifies them in one of five conditions:

  • Normal
  • COVID-19
  • Bacterial Pneumonia
  • Viral Pneumonia
  • Tuberculosis

This is meant to be used in conjunction with a COVID-19 test (or that for an equivalent disease), not as a sole method of diagnosis. In our testing, this model was correct in every COVID-19 prediction it made and had an 80 percent validation accuracy overall.

How Do I Use It?

Click on the Classify tab, upload a lung X-Ray scan and wait for results. The final prediction will be returned along with probability values for every one of the five conditions.
